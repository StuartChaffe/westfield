Let us pay you back even faster

We’ve listened to your feedback and have made our online claims process simpler and quicker. If you have a Good4you, Advantage, Mosaic, Foresight or Chamber plan you can now claim online.

Submit your next dental, optical, chiropody or therapy treatment claim online, and we’ll process your claim within one working day and arrange payment directly into your bank account.
